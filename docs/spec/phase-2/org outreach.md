Hi [name],

I'm founding a worker-owned household services cooperative in Los Angeles — a California cooperative corporation where the cleaners, nannies, and household workers are the legal owners, on W-2 payroll with workers' comp, paid sick leave, and a share of the surplus they vote on.

I'm not writing to ask for an endorsement. I'm writing because your members know things I can't know, and I'd rather be corrected now than after someone's livelihood depends on it. Fifteen minutes by phone with you or anyone on your team would be genuinely valuable, and blunt criticism is the most useful thing you could give me. If it's easier, I have a short note in English and Spanish you're welcome to pass to any members who'd talk to me — I'm compensating workers $30 for a half-hour conversation, because I'm asking for their expertise.

Thank you for the work you do. It's the reason a model like this is even conceivable.
